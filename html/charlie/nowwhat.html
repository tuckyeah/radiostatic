CHARLIE WAITS

I kept watch outside of June's apartment day and night, but after three days with no sign of her - and no sign of anyone looking - I began to worry.

I tried everything I could think of. I called her office, but they just told me to tell her she was fired if I saw her before they did.

She hadn't missed a day of work in almost five years, and that's what they said.

Finally, on the fifth day, I put on my old UPS uniform and snuck past the doorman into her building.

Standing outside her door while I jimmied the lock, I could feel that she wasn't there, but I went in anyway...
