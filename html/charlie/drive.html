I sat in my car until the sun went down and the stars came out, just staring at the horizon.

I'd never driven past that horizon - I'd never been further than a few hours east, I'd never even left the state. But June was out tehre somewhere. Or at least she wasn't here. We had never met but it seemed I may be the only person on the planet who noticed she was missing. Was I going to just and let another opportunity pass me by?

Then the radio turned on, though I hadn't touched it. It made me jump in my seat.

I watched the scanner scroll through the static until it found a voice in the midst of the noise...
