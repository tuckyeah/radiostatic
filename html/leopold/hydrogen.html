Before my very eyes, I am consumed by the fires of Life, of Time, of the Great World burning.

I am returned to what I am, what I was, what I always should be.

As I watch, my hands fade to ash and I realize I am, we are, nothing more than just that: ash and dust.

There is nothing left to be afraid of, My Dearest Sweet Jeremiah.

When it comes down to it,

we are all just stardust and water.
