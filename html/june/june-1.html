June Variable

We'd been driving through the desert for what felt like an eternity. The car was sticky hot.

Charlie and I were both admittedly irritable from spending too long together in such close quarters, so when we saw that Airstream glowing on the horizon,

I genuinely almost expected it to fade into thin air as we approached, like a desert oasis from one of those old Loony Toons.

Before I could even climb out of the car, my partner called my name.


"This is it, June!" he declared with delight. "Or at least, we're close... closer than we've ever been."

"I can feel it in the air, June. We'll find an answer here."


Charlie's smile was so bright it glowed brighter than the trailer. I saw a light in his eyes that I thought long since extinguished, and I have not seen him this giddy in ages.


Who was it that said 'hope springs eternal'?
