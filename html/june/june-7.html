June Variable

Charlie's gone.

I should have expected as much, but I was too distracted by his outburst yesterday - which, of course, should have been my first warning sign that he would do something rash.

But he's never disappeared before... how was I to know this time would be different?

I woke shortly after sunrise and the first thing I did was search through his tent.

Everything was here, even the lantern was still burning its dull orange glow. Wherever he had gone, he had headed there in the dark with nothing but the clothes on his back and the shoes on his feet.

His sleeping bag and pillow were cool and untouched, as if he he had never been there at all.

I lifted his pillow and felt a lump underneath the cloth...
